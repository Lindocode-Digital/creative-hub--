SHORT STORIES

Defined in the dictionary as works of fiction that are shorter than a novel.

I've always been fascinated by fiction, especially by how much of it can be seen as fact and how much as fabrication. When attempting to write good fiction, you always walk a thin line between the two. It’s like trying to be a skilled liar: tell too many facts, and you risk revealing the entire truth; add too much fabrication, and your lie becomes obvious. The key is to find an equilibrium between the two. Below, I make an effort at weaving good lies, and I start small.

– Sdrow Vieli ©
